Our Philosophy

When I started FileBank in my parent's garage several years ago I had three goals in mind; the best security, the biggest storage and the lowest cost.

I'm proud to say that we have achieved those goals and can now offer you what I consider the best place on the internet to store your precious files. As our lives become more reliant on technology, FileBank can be trusted to store your digital assets and memories.

I hope you enjoy the simplicity and convenience of FileBank's cloud storage and join us as we continue on our journey.

Filebank offers the most secure online storage solution to thousands of consumers and hundreds of business around the world. Our highly secure, reliable and user-friendly service provides our customers with convenient storage for their valuable files and digital assets
